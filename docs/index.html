How it works

Three simple moments

1

A gentle chime plays

At regular intervals, a soft tone plays and your screen edges glow. A quote appears. Time to check in.

2

Slide to the edge

Move your mouse to the very left edge for "present" or the very right edge for "not present." Hold for two seconds. Feel it fill.

3

Your rhythm adapts

Stay present and the interval grows. Drift and it shortens. Over time, Zen finds your natural attention span.
